What can creativity do for you?

With innovation and ideas at the heart of business today, creativity is one of the most desirable attributes in a modern career. Innovation is a much-coveted capability that many businesses seek (and struggle) to build. Innovation is the organisation output from creativity by its people - in other words, for companies to innovate, they need creative individuals.
